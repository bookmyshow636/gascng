Who Can Open a CNG Gas Agency?

Starting a CNG gas agency is a promising business opportunity in today’s eco-conscious market. Government-authorized gas distribution companies like IGL, MGL, GAIL, and others lay down certain eligibility norms to ensure that dealerships are awarded to reliable and capable entrepreneurs.

Personal Documents for Indian Citizens and NRIs

Indian citizens aged 21 to 60 years are eligible to apply.
NRIs may also apply by appointing a local manager or authorized representative.

  • Age Proof (Any educational certificate)
  • Income Proof (IT Return)
  • ID Proof (PAN Card)
  • Address Proof (Driving License)

    • Business Documents

  • Income Proof (IT Return / Balance Sheet)
  • Net Worth Certificate from CA
  • Shop and Establishment Certificate

    • Financial Eligibility

    Applicants must demonstrate financial capability with assets typically ranging from ₹20 lakh to ₹50 lakh, depending on the location and scale of the outlet.

    Land / Property Documents

    The applicant must own or lease land compliant with gas company requirements, including size (usually 700–1500 sq. meters), road connectivity, and safety clearances.

    • Property Card
    • Latest Sanctioned DP Remarks from BMC / Local Municipal Office
    • Key Architect Plan Showing Dimensions of Property
    • NOC from the Landlord in Case of Leasehold Land
    • Title Clearance Certificate and Non-Encumbrance Certificate from a Reputed Solicitor (if required)
    • Lease Deed (if applicable)
    • Succession Certificate / Probate of Will from Hon’ble Court (if the original Owner is deceased)
    • Mutation Entry – Ferfar (if Property is Purchased / Sale / Gift / Distribution)
    • Power of Attorney (if required)
    • Latest 7/12 Extract (should be less than 6 months old)

    Other Documents

  • Age Limit: As of the date of application, the applicant must be at least 21 years old and not more than 60 years old
  • Residence: The applicant must be a resident of India as per the Income Tax Act 1962
  • Compliance: All candidates must fulfill the bylaws of the authority
  • Land Dispute: The land where the pump is to be established must be free from any disputes
  • Ownership: The main document for the pump must be in the name of the applicant
  • Location: The CNG station should be in a location well-connected to highways or main roads
  • Land Documentation: The land used for the CNG pump must have valid, complete documents and a land map

    Process to Apply for a CNG Pump

    First Identify Land (Minimum 1500 sq. ft.) for Service

    To open a CNG pump station, you must first identify suitable land. The plot should be at least 1500 sq. ft. to accommodate the station setup and customer vehicles. Choose a location along busy highways, arterial roads, or near existing petrol pumps for better visibility and higher footfall. It's recommended to secure the property on a long‑term lease.

    Site Clearance and NOC Requirements

    Before applying for a CNG Pump license, you must obtain site clearance from various authorities. The following NOCs and approvals are required:

    • ✅ Fire Safety Clearance Certificate
    • ✅ Pollution Control Board Consent
    • ✅ District Magistrate or Commissioner Approval
    • ✅ Electricity Board Feasibility Clearance
    • ✅ Gram Panchayat NOC (if the site is in a village area)

    CNG Dealership through OMC

    An OMC (Oil Marketing Company) is the authorized entity through which you can obtain a CNG dealership. To apply, you must submit the required site clearances along with the application form and applicable fees. The OMC will review your application based on its eligibility criteria and, if approved, will issue a Letter of Intent (LOI).

    Fulfil LOI Conditions (Letter of Intent)

    The Letter of Intent (LOI) outlines certain terms and conditions that must be fulfilled by the applicant within a stipulated time frame. These requirements include:

    • ✅ Registering as a company
    • ✅ Providing land documents
    • ✅ Paying the required security deposit
    • ✅ Getting an electric connection
    • ✅ Arranging necessary financing

    Once these LOI conditions are satisfied, the OMC will proceed to issue the dealership agreement.
